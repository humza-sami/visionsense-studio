What the numbers mean

Three resources compete; whichever fills first is your ceiling. Decode (NVDEC video chip) scales with pixels×fps and is usually the first wall. Compute (GPU cores) only binds for heavy models at high detect fps. VRAM holds the models + per-camera buffers.

detect fps ≠ camera fps · you choose how often the AI looks (2 fps is plenty for alerts) while video stays 25–30 fps.
